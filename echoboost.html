04 — How It Works
Spend that knows
what it's amplifying.

EchoBoost doesn't run cold ads. It deploys spend against products and content that have already been validated by the network — at the exact moment the amplification window is open.

01 — Signal Trigger
Only amplifies proven content
EchoBoost only activates when EchoSignal has flagged an amplification window — a product whose click velocity has meaningfully exceeded its conversion baseline. Spend is never deployed on cold or unvalidated products.
02 — Routing Lock-in
Best merchant baked in at launch
Before a campaign goes live, EchoRoute has already determined the highest-EPC destination. Every ad click lands on the retailer with the best commission rate and conversion rate for that audience — locked in at campaign build time.
03 — Campaign Build
Brands or affiliates can self-serve
Brands fund campaigns directly against a specific window. Affiliates can self-boost their own top content. Both modes use the same ad builder — four steps from product selection to campaign launch across Meta, TikTok, and beyond.
🚀
Signal-led spend outperforms cold spend
By deploying against products with confirmed click velocity and conversion data, EchoBoost campaigns consistently deliver well above 4× ROAS — because they're amplifying what's already working, not testing from zero.
