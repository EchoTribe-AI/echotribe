EchoSignal doesn't aggregate vanity metrics. It watches click velocity, conversion rates, and content performance across the entire affiliate network simultaneously.

Every affiliate signal, one feed
Creator social posts, publisher content pages, and smart link click data — all ingested into a unified signal model. Social creators and content publishers are covered simultaneously, with no manual tagging required.
02 — Scoring
Ranked by real conversion weight
Each product receives a composite score combining click velocity, units ordered, and content engagement — weighted by what actually drives revenue. Scored 0–100. Refreshed every 48 hours across the full network.

When a product's click velocity exceeds its baseline conversion rate by a meaningful threshold, EchoSignal flags an amplification window — the exact signal EchoBoost needs to deploy spend at the right moment, not after.
