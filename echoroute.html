02 — How It Works
The right retailer,
every single click.

EchoRoute replaces the default "link to Amazon" reflex with a real-time decision engine that evaluates every available retailer for that product and that audience before every click resolves.

01 — Signal Input
EchoSignal feeds the top products
EchoRoute only operates on what EchoSignal has already confirmed is outperforming. It doesn't route cold traffic — it maximizes EPC for products with proven conversion velocity, starting from the highest-scoring items.
02 — Routing Logic
CVR × commission rate, per audience
Every product click is evaluated across all available retailers. The engine cross-references that affiliate's historical CVR by retailer, the available commission rate, and real-time inventory to select the optimal destination link.
03 — EPC Delivery
More earned per click, automatically
The affiliate receives a single smart link. EchoRoute handles the routing decision invisibly — detecting device, geography, and retailer availability — and delivers the highest-EPC destination without any manual configuration.
